How Lively Works

Start streaming in under a minute with these simple steps. No complicated setup required.

1

Create Your Stream

Click the "Start Streaming" button and choose your source - webcam, screen share, or media file. Customize your stream settings if needed.

2

Get Your Shareable Link

Once your stream is active, Lively generates a unique link that you can share via email, social media, or messaging apps.

3

Go Live & Interact

As viewers join, communicate with them through live chat and see their reactions in real-time. Control your stream settings at any point.
